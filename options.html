FAQ

How can I blacklist a website?

Websites are blacklisted based on the filter lists settings. You can add and remove default and customized filter lists in your user panel. In addition to that there are two ways how to blacklist a whitelisted website. When you are on a whitelisted website that you want to blacklist, you either simply click on the Adblock Ca$h icon in your browser and switch off the “Whitelist mode” or directly blacklist this website in your user panel. In both cases this website will be removed from your personal whitelist and added to the whitelistable websites section in your user panel.

What types of ads are blocked by Adblock Ca$h?

Adblock Ca$h blocks all types of ads incl. text, banner, video, pop-under and many more.

Besides ads, what else is blocked by Adblock Ca$h?

In addition to blocking ads, Adblock Ca$h blocks malware, tracking and social media buttons that might harm your computer and/or privacy. Adblock Ca$h allows you to browse safer, smoother and more privately through the internet.

FAQ

Do I need an Adblock Ca$h user account to participate in the monthly prize draws?

Yes. Only registered Adblock Ca$h users can collect CashCoins that entitle them to participate in our monthly prize draws. Please note that you do not have to enter your PayPal or bank account information unless you win in the prize draws.

What happens if I have other adblockers besides Adblock Ca$h installed on my computer?

In that case the core functions of Adblock Ca$h (e.g. adblocking and whitelisting) still work, but the special features like collecting CashCoins, participating in prize draws and unlocking exclusive rewards are disabled for as long as you have not disabled all other adblockers besides Adblock Ca$h on your computer.

How do I know if all other adblockers are disabled and I am collecting CashCoins?

If all other adblockers are not disabled you will receive a short notification on your screen and in your Adblock Ca$h popup. Just click on it and it will forward you to the extensions/add-ons store where you can disable the other adblockers.  

Prize draws

How many Reward Categories exist?

Four.

What are the Reward Categories?

There are 4 different Reward Categories. Based on the CashCoins you have collected you will be placed in a Reward Category.

Every Reward Category has its own prize draws and prize money. In the table below you find an overview of the Reward Categories and prize money.

Reward Category % of ABC users Prize Money
1st Top 25% 100 $
2nd Next 25% (26% - 50%) 50 $
3rd Next 25% (51% - 75%) 25 $
4th Next 25% (76% - 100%) 10 $

Do I have the same chance to win in each category?

Yes. Only the prize money differs from category to category.

How can I advance to a higher category?

By enabling whitelisting and visiting the whitelisted websites frequently. To increase your chances we recommend to do both.

Who can participate in prize draws?

Every user aged 18 and older with a CashCoins balance larger than zero.

How frequently do the prize draws take place?

The prize draws take place on the 1st day of each month.

How are the prize draw winners selected?

Our system determines the prize draw winner randomly to guarantee fair and unbiased draws. So all participants have the same chance.

When is it decided what prize draw category I am in?

Your category is “frozen” on the last day of each month at 23:59:59 CET before it is reset.

How am I going to be notified when I win?

We will notify you by email and by notification in your user panel and browser extension

How do I get my prize money when I win?

We will pay out the prize money to your bank or PayPal account depending on the payout option you have chosen in your account under Settings.

What happens after the prize draws winners have been announced?

Your CashCoins balance will be reset.
