Sweepstakes

How many Reward Categories exist?

Four.

What are the Reward Categories?

There are 4 different Reward Categories. Based on the CashCoins you have collected you will be placed in a Reward Category.

Every Reward Category has its own sweepstakes and price money. In the table below you find an overview of the Reward Categories and prize money.

Reward Category % of ABC users Prize Money
1st Top 25% 100 $
2nd Next 25% (26% - 50%) 50 $
3rd Next 25% (51% - 75%) 25 $
4th Next 25% (76% - 100%) 10 $

Do I have the same chance to win in each category?

Yes. Only the price money differs from category to category.

How can I advance to a higher category?

By enabling whitelisting and visiting the whitelisted websites frequently. To increase your chances we recommend to do both.

Who can participate in sweepstakes?

Every user aged 18 and older with a CashCoins balance larger than zero.

How frequently do the sweepstakes take place?

The sweepstakes take place on the 1st day of each month.

How are the sweepstake winners selected?

Our system determines the sweepstake winner randomly to guarantee fair and unbiased draws. So all participants have the same chance.

When is it decided what sweepstake category I am in?

Your category is “frozen” on the last day of each month at 23:59:59 CET before it is reset.

How am I going to be notified when I win?

We will notify you by email and by notification in your user panel and browser extension

How do I get my price money when I win?

We will pay out the price money to your bank or PayPal account depending on the payout option you have chosen in your account under Settings.

What happens after the sweepstakes winners have been announced?

Your CashCoins balance will be reset.
